/// About Digitekt

A founder-led digital partner with roots in Bergen.

Digitekt is the trade name of Sahin Digital, founded by Mustafa Sahin to help small and growing companies become more capable online and behind the scenes.

About Digitekt

Digitekt offers services across web development, system development, IT operations, communication, and design. The goal is simple: give companies a reliable digital partner who can see the whole chain, not only one isolated task.

That can mean a modern website, a custom platform, a smoother internal workflow, safer hosting, clearer communication, or a practical AI-assisted process. The work is shaped around the business, not forced through a template.

Our History

Digitekt began with a practical ambition: help local small businesses digitize their operations and make technology less confusing. Early work focused on websites, IT support, and hands-on digital problem solving.

Over time, that grew into production LMS platforms, WordPress integrations, custom dashboards, mobile apps, automation, and AI research. The same curiosity is still the engine: learn carefully, build responsibly, and improve the way clients work.
