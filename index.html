Common Questions

04 // FAQ
How much does a project cost?
Every project is different — a landing page and a full platform are worlds apart. Reach out with a rough idea of what you want and I'll come back with a free scope call and a fixed quote. No surprises, no billable discovery calls.
How long does it take?
A simple marketing site: 2–3 weeks. A custom web app or LMS-style platform: 6–12 weeks, depending on scope. Mobile apps typically 8–16 weeks. I give you a realistic timeline up front and stick to it.
Do you work with clients outside Bergen?
Yes — I work remotely with clients across Norway and internationally. I'm based in Bergen, but most of my current work is delivered remotely with regular video calls and shared project tracking.
Can you take over an existing project?
Absolutely. I've inherited codebases from other developers and agencies, cleaned them up, and kept them running. Let me audit what you have first — then we decide whether to continue, refactor, or rebuild.
What about ongoing support after launch?
Launch is the start, not the end. I offer monthly maintenance plans that cover hosting, updates, backups, security patches, and a set number of support hours. Or we can work hourly when you need help — your call.
Am I working with a team or just you?
Just me — that's a feature. You get direct contact, fast decisions, and no one translating your requirements through three layers of project managers. Sahin Digital is a deliberately small studio so the work stays personal and the quality stays high.
