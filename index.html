Common Questions

04 // FAQ
How much does a project cost?+
Every project is different — a landing page and a full platform are worlds apart. Reach out with a rough idea of what you want and we will come back with a free scope call and a fixed quote.
How long does it take?+
A simple marketing site: 2–3 weeks. A custom web app or LMS-style platform: 6–12 weeks, depending on scope. Mobile apps typically 8–16 weeks.
Do you work with clients outside Bergen?+
Yes. We work remotely with clients across Norway and internationally. Bergen is home, but most work is delivered through video calls, shared project tracking, and clear written updates.
Can you take over an existing project?+
Yes. We can audit what you have, stabilize it, continue development, or advise whether a refactor or rebuild is the better route.
What about ongoing support after launch?+
Launch is the start, not the end. We offer maintenance, hosting guidance, security updates, backups, and support hours when you need them.
Who is behind Digitekt?+
Digitekt is a small, founder-led studio in Bergen. You work directly with Mustafa Sahin, with trusted specialists involved when a project needs broader capacity.
